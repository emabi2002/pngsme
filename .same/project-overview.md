# PNG SME Marketplace - Project Overview

## Vision
A national digital marketplace & support platform for PNG SMEs/MSMEs (formal and informal) to sell, buy, receive payments, manage their businesses, and access support services within PNG.

## Priority Niche for MVP
1. Agriculture & fresh produce (farmers, market mamas, food suppliers)
2. Handicrafts & locally made products (bilums, carvings, arts, clothes)
3. Retail & services (small shops, tradesmen, micro logistics)

## Core User Roles
1. **Buyer/Customer** - Purchase goods and services
2. **Seller/SME/MSME** - Formal and informal sellers
3. **Logistics Partner** - Delivery providers
4. **SME Council Admin** - Membership and program management
5. **Platform Admin** - Full system control
6. **Support & Training Officer** - Training content management

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend**: Next.js 15, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui
- **Backend**: Supabase (PostgreSQL + Auth + Storage)
- **Payments**: Phase 1 - Manual/COD, Phase 2 - BSP Pay integration
- **Hosting**: Vercel/Netlify
- **Storage**: Supabase Storage

## Phase 1 MVP Scope (3-4 Months)
- User & business onboarding
- Product & service listings
- Search, browse, cart, orders
- Manual/COD payments
- Membership module
- Seller dashboards & receipts
- Admin back office

## Revenue Model
- Vendor subscription fees
- Commission per sale
- Advertisement slots
- Featured product listings
- Training fees
- Payment processing fees
- Delivery commissions
